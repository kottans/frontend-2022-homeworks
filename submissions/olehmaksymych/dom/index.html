    • Manchester United

    Manchester United is one of the most successful English clubs in terms of trophies of all time, and for the last 20 years, the most successful club in England.
    The Red Devils have won 20 FA Cups and three Uefa Champions League Cups. In total, Manchester has 43 titles.

    Bayern Munich

    FC Bayern München is a German football club from Munich. One of the most famous and strongest clubs in Germany and the world.
    Bayern Munich has 84 trophies, 32 of which are Bundesliga cups and 6 UEFA Champions League cups.

    Juventus

    Juventus Football Club is a football club from the city of Turin, one of the oldest clubs in Italy, and one of the most titled clubs in Europe and the world.
    This club has a record 36 Serie A cups, as well as 2 UEFA Champions League cups.
    In total, Juve has total of 75 prestigious trophies.

    Real Madrid

    This club is the best club in Spain in the history of Spanish football.
    The assets of this club include 99 trophies. The club became the champion of "La Liga" 35 times and won the Champions League the maximum number of times - 14.
    Real Madrid is recognized as the best football club of the 20th century according to FIFA.

    PSG

    PSG has a total of 48 trophies.
