The Hardkiss

The HARDKISS is a Ukrainian musical, English-speaking and Ukrainian-speaking rock band, created in 2011 . All the band's songs were written by the soloist Yulia Sanina and Valery Bebko , who is also the creative producer of "The Hardkiss". The Hardkiss are representatives of world-class quality modern Ukrainian music. The audience and critics have repeatedly recognized "The Hardkiss" as the best rock band and the best concert band of the post-Soviet countries and Eastern Europe A feature of the band is the soloist 's bright images and wigs , which are worked on by stylists Slava Chaika and Vitaly Datsyuk .

Imagine Dragons

Imagine Dragons is a pop-rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada. They gained exposure after the release of their debut studio album, Night Visions, in September 2012. Since then, they have gone on a world tour, and gained exposure on major late night shows like The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, Jimmy Kimmel Live! and Late Night with Jimmy Fallon. Billboard magazine named them "The Breakthrough Band of 2013", and Rolling Stone magazine called their single Radioactive "the biggest rock hit of the year."

Nirvana

Nirvana is an American rock band formed by vocalist and guitarist Kurt Cobain and bassist Chris Novoselic in Aberdeen, Washington, in 1987. Several drummers changed in the team; drummer Dave Grohl, who joined Cobain and Novoselic in 1990, played with the band longer than anyone else .

In 1989, Nirvana became part of the Seattle music scene, releasing their debut album Bleach on the indie label Sub Pop. After signing to major label DGC Records, Nirvana had a surprise hit with the song "Smells Like Teen Spirit" from their second album , Nevermind, released in 1991. Later, Nirvana entered the music mainstream, popularizing the alternative rock subgenre called grunge. In the eyes of the media, Kurt Cobain became not just a musician, but the "voice of a generation", and Nirvana became the flagship of "Generation X". In 1993, the third and last studio album of the group, In Utero, was released, the compositions of which were musically very different from the previous works of the group.

Maneskin

Maneskin is an Italian rock band from Rome, consisting of vocalist Damiano David, bassist Victoria de Angelis , guitarist Thomas Raji and drummer Ethan Torcchio. The band members first met as high school students in Monteverde , Rome . The band rose to prominence after finishing as runners-up in the eleventh season of the Italian talent show The X Factor in 2017. In 2021, the band won the music festival in Sanremo and represented Italy at Eurovision 2021 with the song"Zitt i e buoni. The group won the Eurovision Song Contest 2021 with 524 points (206 from the jury and 318 from the audience).

The Beatles

The Beatles were an English rock band, formed in Liverpool in 1960, that comprised John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison and Ringo Starr. They are regarded as the most influential band of all time and were integral to the development of 1960s counterculture and popular music's recognition as an art form.Rooted in skiffle, beat and 1950s rock 'n' roll, their sound incorporated elements of classical music and traditional pop in innovative ways; the band later explored music styles ranging from ballads and Indian music to psychedelia and hard rock. As pioneers in recording, songwriting and artistic presentation, the Beatles revolutionised many aspects of the music industry and were often publicised as leaders of the era's youth and sociocultural movements.
